Wangari Maathai

(1940-2011)

Background Information

  • She was born on 1st April in 1940 in Nyeri
  • 1948- Joined Primary School
  • 1956- Joined Loreto High School in Limuru, the only catholic high school for girls in Kenya
  • 1960- She was granted a scholarship under the Kennedy Airlift to study at Mount St. Scholastica College in the United states
  • 1964- Graduated with a bachelor of science degree
  • 1966- She received her Masters in biological sciences which was funded by the Africa-America Institute
  • 1966- Worked at University College of Nairobi
  • 1969- She got married to the late Mwangi Mathai
  • 1974- She founded Envirocare Ltd., a business that was involved the planting of trees to conserve the environment.
  • 1975- She became appointed to senior lecturer in anatomy, chair of the Department of Veterinary Anatomy becoming the first woman in Nairobi to be appointed to any of these positions
  • 1982 She faced a lot of opposition from the Moi regime as she tried to run for a parliamentary seat
  • 1992- She led a demo to protest the construction of a 60-storey Kenya Times Media Trust Complex in Uhuru Park that left her in a coma for a few days

Awards and Recognitions

  • 1986- Better World Society
  • 1991- Goldman Environmental Prize
  • 1993- Jane Addams Leadership Award
  • 2004- Nobel Peace Prize
  • 2006- Doctor of Public Service
  • 2007- Indira Gandhi Prize
  • 2009- NAACP mage Award- Chairman's Award
  • 2013- Doctor of Science ,Syracuse University, New York
